Ranking of my top 3 movies

Batman

Watching a condensed version of Batman can be a time-efficient way to get a glimpse into the character's world. Whether you have limited time, want a quick introduction to the character, need to catch up on the storyline, or simply have a general interest in Batman, a summarized version can provide key plot points and character developments in a shorter timeframe. While it may not offer the same depth as watching the full content, it can serve as a convenient option to familiarize yourself with Batman's adventures and mythology.

Spiderman

Spider-Man is an iconic superhero with engaging stories, unique powers like web-slinging, and a memorable rogues' gallery of villains. The character's coming-of-age themes and cultural impact make Spider-Man a must-watch for fans and newcomers alike.

Superman

Superman, the symbol of hope, possesses superhuman abilities and engages in epic battles. His compelling backstory and iconic villains add depth to his character. With a significant cultural impact, watching Superman showcases a hero who embodies the best of humanity and inspires with his extraordinary powers and unwavering commitment to justice.
